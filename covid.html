FAQ

When should I stay home from work or school?

If you have fever, vomiting/diarrhea, or you feel too unwell to participate normally, stay home and follow local guidance. Return when symptoms improve and fever has resolved without fever-reducing meds.

What’s the best way to reduce respiratory spread?

Improve ventilation, wash hands, cover coughs, and consider masking if you’re symptomatic or in crowded indoor spaces.

How do I verify information?

Prefer official public health sources (local health department, CDC, WHO) and your clinician for personalized advice.
